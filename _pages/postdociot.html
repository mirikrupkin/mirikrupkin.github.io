  • Opinion and Guides
    • Gender imbalance in academia and STEM: Visibility, causes, solutions, and future, 2018 academic committee report of the council for promoting women in STEM, the Israeli ministry of science and technology. [In Hebrew].
    • Writing a recommendation letter? Use a Gender Bias Calculator to identify gender biases in recommendation letters.
    • What black scientists want from colleagues and their institutions, 6 acedemics share their insight and experience.

    • Progress toward gender equality in the United States has slowed or stalled, England et al. 2020, PNAS.

    • Discrimination against Queer Women in the U.S. Workforce: A Résumé Audit Study, Mishel, 2016, Socius.
    • Opinion: In the wake of COVID-19, academia needs new solutions to ensure gender equity, Malisch et al. 2020, PNAS.
